## Campaign Strategy Summary

This campaign positions Lalo Mavi as **The Legal Algorist** — an Israeli lawyer who doesn't just use AI tools, but leads them strategically. The campaign owns a single defensible concept: in a world where AI is commoditizing legal advice, competitive advantage belongs to lawyers and clients who understand *how* to direct AI, not just how to prompt it.

The strategy is **inbound-first**: instead of pushing sales messages, we publish content that creates genuine intellectual tension — posts that make lawyers uncomfortable, real estate developers pause, and property owners realize they've been underprotected. Every post is proof-of-work for Lalo's expertise.

**Three strategic objectives:**

1. **Authority Building** — Establish Lalo as the leading Israeli voice on AI + Law integration and Pinui-Binui legal complexity.
2. **Trust Building** — Use real scenarios (anonymized), behind-the-scenes work, and transparent thinking to build a relationship before the audience needs a lawyer.
3. **Lead Generation** — Drive DMs and consultation bookings via clear, low-friction CTAs embedded naturally in every post.

## Lead Conversion Funnel: Post → CTA → DM → Call

```
STEP 1 — POST GOES LIVE (07:30 IST)
    Hook stops the scroll in the first 2 lines
    Post body delivers genuine value / emotional resonance
    ↓

STEP 2 — ENGAGEMENT TRIGGERED
    Reactions, comments, shares, saves
    Lalo replies to every comment within 2 hours (algorithm fuel)
    ↓

STEP 3 — CTA ACTIVATES WARM READER
    "שלח לי הודעה עם המילה [KEYWORD]"
    OR: "תגיב בתגובות [WORD]"
    OR: "קישור בתגובה הראשונה" (for articles)
    ↓

STEP 4 — DM RECEIVED
    Lalo responds within 4 hours using DM template (see lead_scripts.md)
    Quick qualification: what's their situation?
    ↓

STEP 5 — CONSULTATION BOOKED
    "30 דקות, חינם, בלי מחויבות"
    Calendly link or WhatsApp scheduling
    ↓

STEP 6 — THE CALL
    DOM-style intake: understand → advise → position value
    Clear next step offered at end of call
    ↓

STEP 7 — CLIENT OR NURTURE
    Converted → engagement letter
    Not ready → 3-message nurture sequence (see lead_scripts.md)
```

**Friction Reduction Principles:**
- CTA is always a single word DM — no forms, no clicks, no friction
- Free consultation removes financial risk from taking the first step
- Every post delivers value first — the CTA feels natural, not like a pitch

## Operational Notes

- All Hebrew posts use heavy white space — max 2–3 sentences per paragraph
